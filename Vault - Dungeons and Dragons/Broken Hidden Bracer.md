A damaged mechanized forearm bracer.
On it's underside lays a blade that is perfectly sharp where it isn't dinged, evidently well taken care of by it's previous owner. On top of the bracer are two slots for darts. The mechanisms seemed to be decayed from time in the mud. The right person could fix either mechanism in (21-1d20) hours.
Once repaired, the blade can be activated as part of a unarmed attack action, needing to be reloaded as a bonus action afterwards. Adds 1d4 piercing damage to an unarmed attack roll. This feature must be used before knowing if the attack was a success, or use a bonus action to activate it afterwards.
Once repaired, one dart can be fired as a bonus as, or 2 as an action, doing 1d4 piercing per hit + any relevant effect damage. 
In order to target two separate creatures while firing two darts on a turn, flurry of blows or a similar feature that grants two attacks in an action/bonus action can be used.
