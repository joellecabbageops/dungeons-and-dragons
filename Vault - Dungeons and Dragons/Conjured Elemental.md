### **2. Conjured Water Elemental**

**Medium Elemental** **Armor Class** 11 + 4 = **15** **Hit Points** 2 + (2 × Essence) → **4 HP** or **6 HP** **Speed** 30 ft., swim 30 ft.

**STR** 16 (+3) | **DEX** 14 (+2) | **CON** 18 (+4) | **INT** 5 (-3) | **WIS** 10 (+0) | **CHA** 8 (-1)

**Damage Resistances** acid **Damage Immunities** poison **Condition Immunities** exhaustion, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone, restrained, unconscious **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10 **Languages** Primordial (Aquan)

**Freeze.** If it takes cold damage, speed reduced by 20 ft. until end of next turn. **Water Form.** Can enter a hostile creature’s space and stop there. Can move through 1-inch spaces without squeezing.

**Actions** **Slam.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 1d8 + 4 bludgeoning damage.
