Gilded Acorns is the Campaign run by Dayna Kelsey Webber.
In the timeline is is set in 1400 [[True Time]]. It features Pleasant Burrows and his search for Gilded Acorns. He comes Across [[Tim the Ranger]], [[Fenrir the Rogue]], [[Scrouge Bungleburger the horse Monk]], [[Hassleman the sorcerer]], [[Daisy the Druid]], [[Madame Boss the Bard]]. Some of them travel through the [[Alpwood Forest]] to [[Maganor]] together and others are found running their conman businesses in [[Maganor]]. They leave [[Maganor]] together in search of dungeons to loot together, with [[Pleasant Burrows]] wanting to teach [[Hassleman the sorcerer]] how to be a good person, and [[Hassleman the sorcerer]] wanting to teach [[Pleasant Burrows]] how money works and find treasure in [[Dungeons]].
