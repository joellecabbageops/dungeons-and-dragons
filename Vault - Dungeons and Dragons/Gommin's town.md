Session run for [[Peter Florance]] and [[Geoff Florance]]

Gommin uses U1 U2 and tunnels around the village connected to the different building around town.

A1: Blacksmith
	There's a black smith in the village run by Gunter Marx, married to Martha Marx. He's had tools go missing from his shop, as well as extra merchandise he's been working, and knows that Bethany who runs the grocery store-front has been losing track of supplies for the last 3 weeks.

A2: Grocery store-front
	Bethany runs the store-front and lots of flours and grains have gone missing alongside small amounts of the villages supply of potatoes. She's seen shadows in the shed, but when she's chased after them they have vanished into thin air in a corner. Needles and threads have been disappearing from her shop. She's definitely worth talking to.

A3: Seamstress
	As Malia roams around her shop, she has similar stories to the other people in the town. As she shows the players the damage, there's a thread unravelled, leading under ground

Gommin is the main threat and the above ground goblins that are attacking the village will leave the town alone and call off the monsters who have also been bothering the village if Gommin is handed over to them.

If they are unable to catch him for whatever reason, or they agree to not send him back to the other Goblins, they will have to fight:
```encounter
creatures:
  - Goblin Minion
  - Goblin Minion
  - Goblin Minion
  - Goblin Warrior
  - Goblin Boss
players:
  - Tameo
  - Aurora
```

The players ended the session with a goblin minion on one HP in their custody and the town guard saw them, surprised at what they had managed. They are going to take the goblin in for questioning.


Session 2 ended with them locking the goblin back up after talking to a Goblin riding a Worg in the forest. They saw the Worgs face, but they never saw the goblin himself.
