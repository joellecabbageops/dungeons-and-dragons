## Features

**[[Bullywug Racial Traits]]**
- **Amphibious.** You can breathe air and water.
- **Standing Leap.** As a bonus action on your turn, you can long jump up to 20 feet without a running start.
- **Swamp Camouflage.** You have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide in swampy or watery terrain.

**[[Protector Domain]] (Cleric 1)**
You gain proficiency with martial weapons and training with heavy armor.

**[[Summon Entity]] (Elemental Plane)**
As an action, summon a [[Conjured Elemental]] within 30 feet. HP = 2 (PB) + (2 × Essence spent). You have 2 Essence.

**[[Direct Control]]**
As an action, command one summoned entity to move up to its full speed.

**Summoned entities** act (move + attack or Dodge) at the end of your turn on your mental command.
