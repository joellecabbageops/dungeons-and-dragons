Kobolds:
	Kobold
		Weak, dagger, scared off when there's no brutes or Captain on the field, terrible fighters. Normally 2ft tall
	Snow Kobold
		Ostracised by normal Kobolds for their scale colour, and take to living in the tops of snow capped mountains together. They are efficient ambush predators. Blue eyes
	Forest Kobold
		Lives in forest and is brown, advantage on stealth checks in the forest.
	Kobold Warrior
		Alright fighters, holding short swords, disadvantage on intimidation checks when there's no Brutes or Captains on the field, between 2 and 2.5 feet tall.
	Kobold Brute
		Around 3.5 ft tall, fist fighters, build like body builders and do multiple melee attacks per turn, very confident
	Kobold Captain
		Carries a long-sword, for a kobold. Around 2.5 to 3ft tall. They have a horn to call for reinforcements as an action. If kobolds are nearby they will run to assist as quickly as possible. Gets the sap ability on it's longsword
	Revered/Reverant Kobold
		Has the ability to sacrifice their shield permanently lowering their AC by 2 for the fight, but raises it by 2 for the turn they use their ability. Their main weapon is a rapier and for a bonus action they can activate it's flame tongue attribute and it will deal an extra 2d6 fire damage.
	Kobold Healer
		Carries a dagger in-case they get into a melee fight. 2x uses of healing word/curewounds from a distance, and unlimited uses of 1d4+2 healing per turn when they touch someone
	Greater Kobold Healer
		Has everything the normal kobold healer has, 1x per day they can focus on healing a target for 60 seconds, healing 1d8+4 health on it's target every time their turn rolls around. ~Concentration
