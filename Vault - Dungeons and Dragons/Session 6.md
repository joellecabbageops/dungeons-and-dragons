[[The tale of seven]]


The players start the session off at Toat the Wizards tower around midday. They will be invited inside for some food, but not yet welcome upstairs.

[[Bandit town in the forest of Doorn]] - Approached by the Ents of the forest, some proclaiming to be a small resistance against their unbeatable foe.

As you walk for the next while, the forest becomes denser, brushier, and thornier, with a type of tree prevailing over the forest and deeper and deeper you go. It's a messy barked moderate sized tree covered in large spikes. You've all heard of these trees, but haven't necessarily seen them. They mark the lands no one has been able to tame, [[The forest of Doorn]].

At some point while they talk, potentially being brought to a halt by forest too dense for the cart to pass, in a semi circle in front of the party, roots stir in front of them, lifting up trees that begin to form their shape into limbs and faces. "We are the trees, big strong and glue" "It's true, not glue" "TRUE!, and we are seeking the help of the soft peoples. We found you, tromping, stomping and rolling. Is it you the soil has provided to restore our health?"

Bandits have set up camp in the centre of the forest under the [[Great Wise One]], an ancient tree of Doorn. In the tops of it's branches it rages against birds shooting spikes at them, though down near it's roots it remains inactive due to the magic of the bandits.
The bandits have access to and the ability to make magic red glass the changes light, magic or not, and disables the alive forest, including the [[Ents of Doorn]].
Some of the [[Ents of Doorn]] are nice, others not, but for the most part they are angry due to what's being done to them by the soft people bandits. Before this point, no one who met them thought they were nice because of how spiked they are.
The Ents, powerless to act on their own are not able to know what causes them to sleep, but they know where the forest is inactive because no one is talking to them.

The Ents bring forth all of the inventory of a group of bandits who were attacked by wild animals.
6 Red capes, 50 gp, 4 plat, 98 sp, 62 cp, 8x swords, three lanterns surrounded by red glass, clothes, boots, a set of keys, ordinary clothes,

The players may want to have the Ents take them to the camp, ambush a group of bandits and take a lantern or a few, sneak inside, whatever.

The Ents want the party to find how they put Ents to sleep, and either report back to make a plan, or disable them all, then and there.

Throughout the bandit camp is 100 people's worth of buildings, men and women in cages, including [[Elvas the Drum Teacher]] and [[Mrs Wyndham]]
