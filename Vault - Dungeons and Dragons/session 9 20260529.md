The players start the session at the point where they separate from the people rescued from the [[Bandit town in the forest of Doorn]], halfway to the [[Seed bank encounter]]. The players rescued the people near sunset, travelled a little way and slept, travelled for a day, slept, part ways and then arrive at the [[Seed bank encounter]] a few hours off of a late summer sunset.

Letters indicate floor of the [[Seed bank encounter]] and numbers indicate the rooms/areas within the floor.

A1 - As you arrive at the digsite, you have mostly left behind the trees of the Doorn forest, though some of their kind are mixed into the ordinary gums you are used to. The sight before you is a clearing with a wide cone shaped hole that heads down to the grey and green stone structure in the center. What you can see of the building stands 8 feet tall carrying a pyramid with a flat topped cap stone. The visible entry way to the building is a narrow square a couple of feet across and tall. The dirt at the entrance has been shovelled away and scrape marks indicate a large number of creatures have squeezed through the opening. Posts with tarps over them and benches under the tarps have work benches with tools made for discovery on top of them. One table has papers scattered across it, and to the edge of the digsite are a few large, destroyed tents.
